Know Us!

Started by a bunch of four foodies, namely Aditya, Ankit, Manasvi and Shruti; we are now a household name in Saddi Dilli and have been featured as the 'Site-to-look-out-for 2015' by Delhi Mirror. We believe in food and food only. Hopefully, this belief will guide us towards serving the ever- growing 'deprived of acha khana population' for ages to come.
